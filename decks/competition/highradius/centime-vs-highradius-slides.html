Competitive Strategy

When do we compete?

HighRadius and Centime rarely compete head-to-head. Different segments, different distribution, different price points. But awareness matters in four scenarios:

Upmarket Mid-Market

$300M–$500M company. Large enough for enterprise, small enough that HR’s overhead hurts. Lead with TCO, speed, and Exchange.

Bank Conversations

HR may surface as a “name brand” ref. HR doesn’t distribute through banks. We do.

AR-to-AP Cross-Sell

Existing HR AR customer considers their AP. Their strongest position. Lead with Exchange and FlexPay.

Verification Evaluation

Buyer specifically evaluating fraud prevention. We win decisively. CAS has no HR equivalent.
